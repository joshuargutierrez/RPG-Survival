=====SIMPLE CHARACTER SELECTOR DOCUMENTATION=====

Hello! Thanks for buying my asset in asset store!
To the addition to my asset you can see 3 characters: cat, human, bull.

If you want to add your characters to this menu:
1) Click to sriptmanager in hierarchy and choose your options in inspector.
2) Select amount of you characters and drag there your prefabs.
3) Don't forget to change scale of scroll. Click to Canvas -> ScrollPanel -> Scroll and change "right value" in inspector.

Code for buttons you can find in Scroll script.
